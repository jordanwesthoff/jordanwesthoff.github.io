About Jordan Westhoff

Hey there, My name is Jordan Westhoff and I am an enthusiastic worker who places value on efficiency, professional delivery and integrity. I have a diverse knowledge of digital video, networking and computational programming with an express interest in how they can collide to develop advances in networking, cinema and imaging science technology.
My work at the Rochester Institute of Technology encompasses quite a diverse range of subjects, from film and digital film productions to radiometry and IT work that involves High Performance Computing and 4K video technologies. In addition to all of this, I am working on my own Senior Thesis project, the Cerberus cluster, in order to complete my degree and graduate this spring.
